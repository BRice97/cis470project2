Key Facts & Importance

Summary

What Is It User Experience, also known as UX, focuses on understanding the user, by knowing what they need, what they value, their abilities, and their limitations.

Key Factors in User Experience Useful, Usable, Desirable, Findable, Accessible, Credible

Why UX is so Important

Response

Ux is a evolving field and is constantly being defined. Considering the user's needs is the most important component to any product because it outlines the important, needs, and functions of the product. Without considering the user's needs, a product would be relatively useless because it would not target its desired audience effectively.
Peter Morville | UX Honeycomb
